3 days in Rishikesh, Uttrakhand

Spend your first day exploring the main tourist attractions in Rishikesh, such as Lakshman Jhula, Triveni Ghat, and the Beatles Ashram. Take a yoga class or visit an ashram to experience the spiritual side of Rishikesh. Enjoy the sunset over the Ganges River. On your second day, go whitewater rafting or bungee jumping for an adrenaline-pumping adventure. Visit the Neer Garh Waterfall for a refreshing break from the city. Relax in a spa or take a dip in the Ganges River. On your third day, hike to Tapovan or Vashishta Gufa for stunning views of the Himalayas. Visit the Parmarth Niketan Ashram for a spiritual experience. Enjoy a farewell dinner at a rooftop restaurant with a view of the river.

Best places in Rishikesh

  • Lakshman Jhula
  • Triveni Ghat
  • The Beatles Ashram
  • Neer Garh Waterfall
  • Tapovan
